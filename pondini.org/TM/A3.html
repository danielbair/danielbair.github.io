A3.  Tinker Tool

Previous        Frequently Asked Questions        Home     Troubleshooting      Contact      Next 

 

Click here to download the free Tinker Tool app.  It allows you to change the Finder to show hidden files (among many other things).  Select the first option under Finder, then click Relaunch Finder at the bottom.  Reverse this when done.


Do not use any of the other options unless you know the possible consequences.
