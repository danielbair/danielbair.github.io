Time Machine - Troubleshooting

B4.  Wrong  Icon  shown for TM drive/partition

Previous        Frequently Asked Questions        Home     Troubleshooting      Contact      Next 

 

If the icon is shown on your Desktop, try de-selecting, then re-selecting the appropriate "Show" option in  Finder > Preferences > General.



If it’s shown in your Finder sidebars, try de-selecting, then re-selecting the appropriate "Show" option in  Finder > Preferences > Sidebar.



Try a "Full Reset" as in item #A4.



Try a Back Up Now  from the Time Machine icon in your Menubar, or by control-clicking (right-clicking) the Time Machine icon in your Dock.
